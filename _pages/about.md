I am pursuing my **M.Sc. in Computer Science** at the **[University of Guelph](https://www.uoguelph.ca/)** with a collaborative specialization in **Artificial Intelligence** through the **[Vector Institute](https://vectorinstitute.ai/)**. I am also working as a Research Assistant at the **[Digital Innovation Lab](https://algomau.ca/research/innovation-labs/)** at **[Algoma University](https://algomau.ca/)** under the supervision of **[Dr. Randy Lin](https://algomau.ca/academics/programs/computer-science/)**. My research interests lie in **Information Retrieval** and **Natural Language Processing**.

During my master's, I am working under the supervision of **[Dr. Yan Yan](https://www.uoguelph.ca/profile/yan-yan)** and **[Dr. Randy Lin](https://algomau.ca/academics/programs/computer-science/)**. My research focuses on **Agentic Information Retrieval for Healthcare**, which I am conducting in collaboration with **[Ontario Health](https://www.ontariohealth.ca/)**.
